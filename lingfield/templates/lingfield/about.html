About Us

Lingfield Pharmacy is an independent delivery only pharmacy based in Darlington, DL1 4QZ. Lingfield Pharmacy offers free Repeat Prescription Service (Prescription Collection & Delivery) to our local customers and nationally. As a distance selling pharmacy we provide a full range of NHS essential services to persons anywhere in England. We providing a reliable and efficient service to your doorstep. We are able to deliver both NHS and Private prescriptions.

Our aim is to make the process of getting your medicines easier and convenient for you. You can order your medication from the comfort of your own home and we'll deliver it to your door for FREE. We have a team of experienced pharmacists who are happy to help and to answer your queries.

We are registered with the General Pharmaceutical Council and also have an NHS contract.

Our goal is simple:
We like to be the best pharmacy. We like to be the best in terms of going that extra mile for our customers, the best NHS service provider and the best place to work. We welcome any general comments about the services we provide and any suggestions of ways in which we can improve our services to you. Just contact us for more information on the full range of services we provide.
